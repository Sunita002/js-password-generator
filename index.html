Generate a Password

  1. Click "Generate Password" and follow the series of prompts that appear on the screen.
  2. Enter your desired password length when prompted.
  3. Choose at least one of the following character types by clicking "OK" when prompted.
    • Lowercase characters
    • Uppercase characters
    • Numbers
    • Special characters (! @ #, etc.)
  4. If you click "Cancel" on the character prompts, the character type will not be included in your password.
